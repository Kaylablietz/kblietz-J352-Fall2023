Book baning in American

Information about the baning of books in American

By Kayla Blietz

A conversation has been going on about banning books in schools for themes that some people do not agree with having those themes in schools. Books are being banned in different states for having different themes that not everyone agrees with having in the schools.Some of the themes are LGBTQ+, sexual content and main content or secondary characters of color. there are different places in schools where books are being banned such as classroom libary and schoool libary.
