Book baning in American

Information about the baning of books in American

By Kayla Blietz

A conversation has been on going about banning books in schools for themes that some people do not agree with having in schools. Books are facing bans in various states due to their themes of LGBTQ+ content, sexual material and books that have a person of color as the main characters or as a secondary characters. there are different places in schools where books are being banned such as classroom libraries and school libraries.
